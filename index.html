About

I am Keren Maidariya Aywila, a self-taught front-end developer from Nigeria. I specialize in turning figma and psd designs to fully functional websites with focus on pixel-perfect, responsiveness and accessibility. I love how users interact with computers through awesome user-interfaces and softwares which led to my software development journey. I freelance most of the time and i take on contract time to time.
